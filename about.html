Our Story

Founded in 2018 in Durban, Coastal Bean Cafe is a family-run coffee shop passionate about serving fairly traded beans and creating a welcoming space for everyone.

Our Values

  • Fair trade sourcing
  • Community engagement
  • Professional, friendly service

Sourcing Process

We partner with smallholder farmers through ethical cooperatives to ensure traceability, quality, and fair prices for our beans. Roasted locally to highlight the best of African coffee.
